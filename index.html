ABOUT
ME

Hi! I'm Vivienne Lam. I've graduated in 2023 with a Bachelors of Design and IT, majoring in Interactive Media at Monash University.I value unique ideas and well thought out design. I believe my strength mainly lies within motion design but I have strong passion for web design where I enjoy implementing design in a way that allows it to be interactive. Below are some of the works that I have really enjoyed through my time in Uni and I hope that you can enjoy viewing them !

Contact me!

MOTION

For a free choice animation project, I developed this motion piece which focuses on a childlike imagination. The plot follows a young girl who is taken to the aquarium where she wanders off and we see her imagination take over.

This motion piece was for the Little Food Festival 2023 and was display at Federation Square for the event. The theme I chose to focus on was pickling which I explain the environmental impact of food waste and the importance of implementing food preservation methods like pickling in the video above.

We were required to develop a brand and create a vector logo animation as well as a one minute promotional video. The brand I created was for a company called 'Cabbage Bound' which is a community farm specialising in growing fresh produce specifically cabbages!

This video required a combination of filmed footage and an overlay utilizing tools in AfterEffects like rotobrush to create an infographic styled video. I chose to do how to do digital frame by frame animation using a frame by frame overlay.
